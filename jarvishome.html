Welcome to Jarvis

Your personal AI assistant, ready to assist with your tasks efficiently.

Here's the commands you can use:

  1. "Hi Jarvis" - Greet Jarvis.
  2. "What can you do?" - List of Jarvis' capabilities.
  3. "Close this" - Close opened popups.
  4. "What's the weather?" - Get a quick weather update.
  5. "Show the full weather report" - Detailed weather report.
  6. "Are you there?" - Check Jarvis' presence.
  7. "Shut down" - Stop voice recognition.
  8. "Open Google" - Opens Google in a new tab.
  9. 'Search for "your keywords"' - Perform a Google search.
  10. "Open YouTube" - Opens YouTube.
  11. 'Play "your keywords"' - Search for videos on YouTube.
  12. "Close this YouTube tab" - Close the opened YouTube tab.
  13. "Open Instagram" - Opens Instagram.
  14. "Connection status" - Check internet connectivity.
  15. "Current time" - Display current time.
  16. "Current charge" - Show battery percentage.
  17. "Charging status" - Check if the device is charging.
